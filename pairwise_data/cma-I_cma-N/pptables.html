The median number of conducted function evaluations is additionally given in italics, if the target in the last column was never reached. Entries, succeeded by a star, are statistically significantly better (according to the rank-sum test) when compared to all other algorithms of the table, with p = 0.05 or p = 10−k when the number k following the star is larger than 1, with Bonferroni correction by the number of functions (24). Best results are printed in bold.
